Welcome to CollabCloud

CollabCloud is an unofficial website for everything concerning the International Baccalaureate. It aims to help students and educators collaborate better in virtual classrooms and online communities.

Features

✔ Connect with IB learners across the globe
✔ Join Discord communities for support and discussion
✔ Stay updated with resources, tips, and IB-related news

About

Virtual classrooms have made it easier for students from different parts of the globe to learn together. They provide access to all kinds of study material that many students can’t access due to several reasons. Even with all kinds of advantages, virtual classrooms also have disadvantages such as a lack of physical interaction and communication challenges. CollabCloud helps overcome those issues through better-designed online tools and community features.
